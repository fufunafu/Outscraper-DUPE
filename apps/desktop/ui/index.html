Filters

A 5.0 from two reviews isn't a good rating. Pair this with a rating filter.

Filters trim the export, they don't speed up the scrape — Google has no filter parameter, so every row gets fetched either way.

After scraping, visits each business's website to pull its email and social links. Adds time (a second or two per site) and only works for places that have a website. Runs from this computer's IP.
